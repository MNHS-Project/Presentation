About Ramon Magsaysay

Full Name: Ramon del Fierro Magsaysay
Date of Birth: August 31 1907
Date of Death: March 19 1957
Cause of Death: Plane crash
In office: December 30 1953 – March 19 1957

Achievements:

Land reform and rural development
Anti-communist campaigns Transparency and good governance Improved public services and infrastructure

Style in Leadership:

He was known for his hands-on Approach, simplicity, and service for Filipino people and thats Why they Call hi

Conclusion:

Magsaysay’s leadership left a lasting mark on Philippine politics, inspiring future leaders to value honesty, transparency, and service to the people.
